# LinkedIn Calendar Selected Topic to Brief - Evaluation Guidelines

## Overview
The LinkedIn Calendar Selected Topic to Brief workflow aims to transform pre-selected LinkedIn topics from content calendars into comprehensive, LinkedIn-optimized content briefs. The workflow loads executive profile and content strategy documents for personalization and strategic alignment, generates detailed LinkedIn-specific briefs incorporating platform best practices and executive positioning, provides human-in-the-loop editing and approval with iteration capabilities, and delivers strategic blueprints for achieving specific business outcomes through LinkedIn content creation.

## LLM Node Evaluation Guidelines

### 1. Brief Generation Node
**Node ID**: `brief_generation_llm`  
**Task Type**: Strategic LinkedIn content brief creation (High complexity, Medium variance)

#### What We Are Analyzing
This node generates comprehensive LinkedIn content briefs from pre-selected topics, incorporating executive profile characteristics, content strategy guidelines, and LinkedIn platform optimization to create strategic blueprints for high-impact content creation.

#### Ideal Output
- Strategic foundation with clear reasoning connecting topic to executive's broader goals
- Comprehensive content architecture with detailed section-by-section structure and reasoning
- Engagement engineering tactics with psychological principles and expected outcomes
- LinkedIn-specific optimization including algorithm considerations and formatting strategy
- Measurable success metrics with clear targets and benchmarks
- Risk mitigation strategies and follow-up opportunities for extended value

#### Evaluation Parameters
1. **Strategic Foundation Quality**: Depth of reasoning connecting topic to business objectives and audience insights
2. **Content Architecture Sophistication**: Quality of structural design with clear reasoning for each section
3. **Engagement Engineering Effectiveness**: Strategic design of engagement tactics with psychological backing
4. **LinkedIn Platform Optimization**: Understanding and application of LinkedIn-specific best practices
5. **Executive Voice Integration**: Incorporation of executive profile characteristics and positioning
6. **Actionability and Clarity**: Specificity of guidance that enables skilled writers to create exceptional content

### 2. Brief Feedback Analysis Node
**Node ID**: `analyze_brief_feedback`  
**Task Type**: Feedback interpretation and strategic revision guidance (Medium complexity, Medium variance)

#### What We Are Analyzing
This node analyzes user feedback on LinkedIn content briefs to provide strategic revisions that enhance effectiveness while respecting user intent and preserving manual edits, ensuring iterative improvement without losing strategic integrity.

#### Ideal Output
- Accurate interpretation of both explicit requests and implicit user needs
- Strategic reasoning explaining why proposed revisions will improve content effectiveness
- Clear, actionable revision instructions that respect user edits and maintain strategic alignment
- Identification of preserved elements including successful original components and user modifications
- Conversational change summary acknowledging user feedback appropriately
- Impact assessment explaining how changes will improve goal achievement

#### Evaluation Parameters
1. **Feedback Interpretation Accuracy**: Correct understanding of both explicit and implicit user requirements
2. **User Edit Preservation**: Appropriate respect for manual modifications while incorporating new feedback
3. **Strategic Integrity Maintenance**: Preservation of strategic value while addressing user concerns
4. **Revision Instruction Clarity**: Specificity and actionability of guidance for brief improvement
5. **Progressive Enhancement**: Building upon previous iterations without regression
6. **LinkedIn Context Preservation**: Maintaining platform-specific optimization through revisions

## General Evaluation Considerations

### Quality Thresholds
- **Critical**: Outputs that fail on more than 2 evaluation parameters
- **Needs Improvement**: Outputs that fail on 1-2 evaluation parameters
- **Acceptable**: Outputs that meet all evaluation parameters with minor issues
- **Excellent**: Outputs that exceed expectations on all parameters

### Evaluation Frequency
- Initial baseline evaluation should be conducted on a sample of 10-20 workflow runs
- Regular spot checks should be performed weekly
- Full evaluation should be repeated after any prompt or schema modifications

### Documentation Requirements
- Record specific examples of failures for each parameter
- Document patterns in errors or quality issues
- Track improvement trends over time
- Maintain a log of suggested improvements and their implementation status

### Workflow-Specific Considerations
- **Executive Profile Dependency**: Quality heavily depends on executive profile completeness and accuracy
- **LinkedIn Algorithm Knowledge**: Requirements for platform-specific optimization and algorithm understanding
- **Iteration Management**: Maximum 10 feedback iterations affects refinement depth and user satisfaction
- **Strategic Complexity**: High-level strategic thinking required for business outcome alignment
- **Manual Edit Handling**: Critical requirement to preserve user modifications while incorporating feedback
- **Voice Preservation**: Maintaining executive's authentic voice and positioning throughout iterations
- **Engagement Engineering**: Sophisticated understanding of psychological principles and LinkedIn user behavior
