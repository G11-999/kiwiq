# Content Studio Workflows

## Overview
Content Studio workflows handle the complete content creation pipeline, transforming user inputs into polished, publication-ready content. These workflows support both blog and LinkedIn content creation, from initial ideation through brief development to final content generation.

## Available Workflows

### Blog Content Workflows

#### 1. Blog User Input to Brief
**File:** `wf_blog_user_input_to_brief.py`
- Transforms user's raw ideas into structured blog content briefs
- Conducts research to enrich the brief with relevant information
- Develops comprehensive content outlines
- Creates SEO-optimized brief with keywords and meta descriptions

#### 2. Blog Brief to Blog
**File:** `wf_blog_brief_to_blog.py`
- Converts content briefs into full-length blog articles
- Maintains brand voice and style consistency
- Incorporates SEO best practices
- Generates publication-ready blog content with proper formatting

#### 3. Blog Calendar Selected Topic to Brief
**File:** `wf_blog_calendar_selected_topic_to_brief.py`
- Takes pre-selected topics from content calendar
- Develops detailed briefs for scheduled blog posts
- Ensures alignment with content strategy
- Prepares briefs for batch content creation

#### 4. Blog Content Calendar Entry
**File:** `wf_blog_content_calendar_entry.py`
- Generates content calendar entries with strategic topics
- Plans content distribution across timeframes
- Aligns topics with business goals and seasons
- Creates organized content publication schedules

#### 5. Blog Content Optimisation Workflow
**File:** `wf_blog_content_optimisation_workflow.py`
- Optimizes existing blog content for better performance
- Enhances SEO elements and readability
- Updates content with current information
- Improves content structure and engagement factors

### LinkedIn Content Workflows

#### 6. LinkedIn User Input to Brief
**File:** `wf_linkedin_user_input_to_brief.py`
- Transforms user ideas into LinkedIn post briefs
- Tailors content for professional audience
- Develops thought leadership angles
- Creates briefs optimized for LinkedIn's algorithm

#### 7. LinkedIn Content Creation Workflow
**File:** `wf_linkedin_content_creation_workflow.py`
- Generates complete LinkedIn posts from briefs
- Crafts engaging hooks and calls-to-action
- Optimizes post length and formatting
- Creates posts designed for maximum engagement

#### 8. LinkedIn Calendar Selected Topic to Brief
**File:** `wf_linkedin_calendar_selected_topic_to_brief.py`
- Develops briefs from pre-planned LinkedIn topics
- Ensures consistent posting schedule
- Aligns with professional branding strategy
- Prepares briefs for systematic content creation

#### 9. LinkedIn Content Calendar Entry
**File:** `wf_linkedin_content_calendar_entry.py`
- Plans LinkedIn content distribution strategy
- Generates strategic posting calendar
- Balances content types and themes
- Creates structured content schedule

#### 10. LinkedIn Alternate Text Suggestion Workflow
**File:** `wf_linkedin_alternate_text_suggestion_workflow.py`
- Generates alternative text variations for posts
- Provides A/B testing options
- Creates diverse content angles
- Optimizes messaging for different audiences

- Audience targeting and persona alignment
- Content structure and outline creation

### Content Generation
- Brand voice and tone consistency
- Platform-specific optimization
- Engagement-focused writing
- Publication-ready formatting

### Content Planning
- Strategic calendar development
- Topic ideation and selection
- Content distribution planning
- Campaign alignment

## Integration Points
Content Studio workflows integrate with:
- **Content Diagnostics:** Use insights to inform content creation
- **Playbook Workflows:** Follow strategic guidelines for consistency
- **Customer Data:** Personalize content based on audience insights

## Output Formats
- **Blog Content:** Full articles with headlines, meta descriptions, and body content
- **LinkedIn Posts:** Optimized posts with hooks, body, and CTAs
- **Content Briefs:** Structured documents with research, outlines, and guidelines
- **Content Calendars:** Strategic schedules with topics and publication dates

## Best Practices
1. Start with diagnostics to understand content gaps
2. Use briefs to maintain consistency across content
3. Leverage calendars for systematic content production
4. Optimize existing content regularly for better performance
