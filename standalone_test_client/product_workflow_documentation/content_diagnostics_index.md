# Content Diagnostics Workflows

## Overview
Content Diagnostics workflows provide comprehensive analysis and assessment of your content's performance, visibility, and competitive positioning across various platforms. These workflows help identify gaps, opportunities, and strategic insights to improve your content strategy.

## Available Workflows

### 1. Blog Content Analysis
**File:** `wf_blog_content_analysis.py`
- Analyzes existing blog content for quality, SEO effectiveness, and engagement metrics
- Evaluates content alignment with target audience and brand voice
- Identifies content gaps and improvement opportunities
- Provides actionable recommendations for blog optimization

### 2. LinkedIn Content Analysis
**File:** `wf_linkedin_content_analysis.py`
- Assesses LinkedIn post performance and engagement patterns
- Analyzes content consistency and professional branding
- Evaluates thought leadership positioning
- Provides insights for LinkedIn content strategy enhancement

### 3. Company AI Visibility Workflow
**File:** `wf_company_ai_visibility_workflow.py`
- Evaluates how AI systems perceive and rank your company's content
- Analyzes company presence in AI-generated responses
- Assesses brand visibility in AI search results
- Identifies opportunities to improve AI discoverability

### 4. Executive AI Visibility
**File:** `wf_executive_ai_visibility.py`
- Analyzes executive team's digital footprint and thought leadership presence
- Evaluates how AI systems represent company executives
- Assesses executive content impact and reach
- Provides strategies for enhancing executive visibility

### 5. Competitor Content Analysis
**File:** `wf_competitor_content_analysis.py`
- Benchmarks your content against competitor strategies
- Identifies competitor content strengths and weaknesses
- Analyzes competitive content gaps you can exploit
- Provides competitive intelligence for content planning

### 6. Deep Research Workflow
**File:** `wf_deep_research_workflow.py`
- Conducts in-depth research on specific topics or industries
- Aggregates insights from multiple sources and perspectives
- Generates comprehensive research reports
- Provides data-driven content recommendations

### 7. LinkedIn Scraping
**File:** `wf_linkedin_scraping.py`
- Extracts and analyzes LinkedIn profile and company page data
- Collects engagement metrics and content performance data
- Builds datasets for LinkedIn content analysis
- Supports data-driven LinkedIn strategy development

### 8. Orchestrator Workflow
**File:** `wf_orchestrator_workflow.py`
- Coordinates multiple diagnostic workflows for comprehensive analysis
- Combines insights from various diagnostic components
- Generates unified diagnostic reports
- Provides holistic content strategy recommendations

### 9. Company Analysis Workflow
**File:** `wf_company_analysis_workflow.py`
- Performs comprehensive company content and positioning analysis
- Evaluates brand messaging consistency across channels
- Analyzes company content performance metrics
- Identifies strategic improvement opportunities

### 10. Company AI Visibility Edge Case
**File:** `wf_company_ai_visibility_edge_case.py`
- Handles special scenarios in AI visibility analysis
- Addresses unique company positioning challenges
- Provides specialized AI visibility strategies
- Manages edge cases in content discovery

## Workflow Integration
These diagnostic workflows can be run independently or in combination to provide comprehensive content insights. The orchestrator workflow is particularly useful for running multiple diagnostics and generating integrated reports.

## Output
All diagnostic workflows generate detailed reports with:
- Current state analysis
- Performance metrics
- Gap identification
- Actionable recommendations
- Priority improvements

## Next Steps
After running diagnostics, results can be used to:
1. Inform content strategy decisions
2. Guide content creation in Content Studio workflows
3. Generate strategic playbooks using Playbook workflows
4. Track improvement progress over time
