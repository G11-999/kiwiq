Research Protocol for High-Value Angel Investors

🎯 CONTEXT & TASK
Mission: Research and score US-based angel investors (0-100) for seed-stage B2B AI/MarTech startup founded by IIT/U-Michigan alumni. (Whatever the total score is, youwill normalize to 0-100 range).
Target Profile: Angels with tier-1 employer brand, functional expertise (marketing/growth/product/eng), strong VC connectivity, active check-writing, and potential shared affinity.
No Disqualifiers: All angels scored, but <40 = skip.

📊 SCORING FRAMEWORK (105 points max, 100 max after normalization)
A. EMPLOYER BRAND & CREDIBILITY (0-25) 🔥 TIER-1 PRIORITY
Current/Former Employer Brand:
  Tier-1 (Google, Amazon, OpenAI, Anthropic, Adobe, HubSpot, Salesforce, Meta, Microsoft, Stripe, Snowflake) = 15 pts
  Tier-2 (Unicorns: Notion, Figma, Databricks, Scale AI, etc.) = 12 pts
  Tier-3 (Series C+ startups, strong brands) = 8 pts
  Tier-4 (Earlier stage or weaker brand) = 4 pts

Role Level at Brand:
  C-level (CMO, CPO, CTO, VP→C-level) = 7 pts
  VP-level = 5 pts
  Director/Senior IC = 3 pts

Ex-Founder Bonus: 🏆
  Founder of successful exit ($50M+) = +10 pts (ADDED TO ABOVE)
  Founder of acquired/failed startup = +7 pts
  Currently founding = +5 pts
Why This Matters:
Signal value: "Backed by ex-CMO of HubSpot" opens doors
Pattern recognition from scaling tier-1 companies
Employer brand = instant credibility with VCs and customers
Queries:
"[Angel]" (Google OR Amazon OR OpenAI OR Anthropic OR Adobe OR HubSpot OR Salesforce) linkedin

"[Angel]" founder OR founded OR "co-founder"

B. FUNCTIONAL EXPERTISE (0-25) 🔥 CRITICAL
Domain Expertise:
  CMO/VP Marketing at B2B scale-up = 15 pts
  VP Sales/Revenue/Growth = 12 pts
  VP Product (AI/ML focus) = 12 pts
  CTO/VP Eng (AI/infra) = 10 pts
  Generalist operator = 5 pts

Relevance to MarTech/AI:
  Direct MarTech experience = 10 pts
  AI/ML product leadership = 8 pts
  B2B SaaS GTM expertise = 7 pts
  Growth/PLG expertise = 6 pts
  Adjacent expertise = 3 pts
Expertise + Brand Stack Bonus:
Ex-CMO at HubSpot = 15 (role) + 15 (brand) = 30 base points
Ex-VP Growth at Google + Founder = 12 + 15 + 7 = 34 points
This creates compounding value for tier-1 operators
Queries:
"[Angel]" (CMO OR "VP Marketing" OR "VP Sales" OR "VP Product")

"[Angel]" MarTech OR "marketing technology" OR "AI product"

C. VC NETWORK ACCESS (0-20) 🎯 HIGH VALUE
VC Ecosystem Integration:
  Co-invests with tier-1 VCs (Sequoia, a16z, Benchmark) = 10 pts
  Co-invests with tier-2 VCs = 7 pts
  Limited VC co-investment = 3 pts

Portfolio Success Signals:
  5+ portfolio cos raised Series A/B = 10 pts
  2-4 portfolio cos raised follow-ons = 7 pts
  1 portfolio co raised follow-on = 4 pts
  No portfolio follow-ons = 0 pts
How to Validate:
Check if portfolio companies raised follow-on rounds (Crunchbase)
Look for co-investment patterns with known VCs
See if they're listed as "advisor" on portfolio company pages
Check if VCs follow them on Twitter/acknowledge them
Queries:
# Portfolio follow-on success
"[Angel]" portfolio site:crunchbase.com "series A" OR "series B"

# Co-investment patterns
"[Angel]" AND (Sequoia OR a16z OR Benchmark OR Greylock OR Accel) invested OR "co-investor"

# Advisor roles (network proxy)
"[Angel]" advisor site:crunchbase.com OR site:linkedin.com

# VC acknowledgment
"[Angel]" site:twitter.com OR site:linkedin.com (Sequoia OR a16z OR Greylock) mentions

D. CHECK ACTIVITY & SIZE (0-20)
Check Frequency (last 24 months):
  15+ angel checks = 10 pts
  8-15 checks = 8 pts
  3-7 checks = 6 pts
  1-2 checks = 2 pts

Typical Check Size:
  $100K+ = 10 pts
  $50K-$100K = 8 pts
  $25K-$50K = 6 pts
  $10K-$25K = 3 pts
  <$10K = 1 pt
Queries:
"[Angel]" angel investor 2024 OR 2025

"[Angel]" announces investment OR backs OR invests site:crunchbase.com

E. SHARED AFFINITY (0-15) 🎯 UNIQUE ADVANTAGE
Educational Connection:
  IIT alumni = 7 pts
  U-Michigan alumni = 7 pts
  Other top school (Stanford, MIT, Berkeley) = 4 pts

Geographic/Cultural:
  Indian/South Asian background = 5 pts
  Lived/worked in India = 3 pts

Technical Background:
  Engineering/CS degree = 3 pts
  Technical + Michigan/IIT = bonus rapport
Queries:
"[Angel]" IIT OR "Indian Institute of Technology"

"[Angel]" Michigan OR "University of Michigan" OR "U-M"

"[Angel]" India OR Indian OR Bangalore OR Mumbai
